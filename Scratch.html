How might we help social sellers gain trust and loyalty from their customers?

Scratch Photos

Understanding Social Sellers

Work at

Shopify

Role

Product Designer

Timeline

May 2020 - June 2020


The Challenge

Social sellers have a lot of competition due to domestic dropshipping similar types of products. With the large amount of competition they find it difficult to attract customers and also show them that they are running a credible business. How might we help these merchants earn their trust and loyalty from their target customers?

  1. 1. Understand how social sellers market their products
  2. 2. Understand what low-cost methods these merchants use to start advertising

Approach


User Research - Understanding Social Sellers

  1. 1. Social sellers are scrappy and they’re looking for new business opportunities. They need low cost tools to make things happen quick, easily and efficiently to help them overcome challenges they face on social media
  2. 2. The whole market is mobile first (Android holds 90% of the market in India)
  3. 3. Most of these sellers are not professional photographers, and cannot afford to hire photographers to help advertise their products. Simplifying this time can help reduce their time to go to market.
  4. 4. Social Sellers are resellers/dropshippers and sometimes do not have the products on hand to take pictures and rely on pictures from the manufacturer.

Competitive Analysis

Canva

paid automatic background removal tool, requires user to have a bit of design knowledge to ensure they are marketing it properly. Pro - has a lot of features/designed to help advertise

PicMonkey

paid automatic background removal tool, similar to Canva

Remove.bg

free and works well, has a brush to remove or add excess - not available mobile

Background erase iOS app

requires user to use a brush and manually remove background. Paid after a free trial.

Solution

Looking at the user research the understanding was we needed to create a mobile first application that was easy to use and can help social sellers go to market as quickly as possible. Since there are a variety of photography editing apps out there we needed to be more solution driven to help understand our audience even better. This is a very large problem to tackle, so our first experiment was to create a mobile first background removal tool that can help them in their branding, and quickly shipping their product into the market.

• Used an AI driven algorithm to remove the background on photos in one click without the user needed to use extra tools to refine their image

•Quickly add a background if needed and any other elements if feasible for the MVP

Results


  1. 1. Over 195K downloads
  2. 2. Over 200K images downloaded

Common User Feedback

Users found the background removal process to be quick and easy and were very happy with the first launch. However, they would love to see more features to be added to help aid their business

Improvements


Target Audience

No. With the way we marketed our tool, most merchants already on Shopify or who were planning to open an online store had a better time using our product especially when it came to uploading on platforms where a white background colour was required. Currently, the target audience is reaching a variety of people wanting to use the background remover for a variety of purposes (i.e., not for product use), which is causing us not to see the full picture of how social sellers are using these apps for their benefit. Next time, we might want to add features that only merchants and social sellers would want to use.

Rebranding

This is currently an MVP where we launched to experiment and see the reaction and feedback we would get from our users. Since we have more information this work is currently being continued with a rebrand to make it consistent with Shopify
