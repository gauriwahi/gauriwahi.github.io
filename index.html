About Me

Hi I'm Gauri!

My name is Gauri and my pronouns are she/her/hers. I have background in Computer Science with an emphasis in Human-Computer Interaction. I started off my journey as a developer and realized my passion was in understanding and learning about the thought process, motivation and feelings that impact the decisions people make. For me, design is a way to discover real world problems and help solve them in a creative and logical way that can help empower users and make a social impact through joyful and engaging experiences.

I also believe that design should be invisible in the sense that users are so drawn into using your product that they don't discover anything that would take that experience away from them.

If you would like to chat and grab a coffee, shoot me an email at gauri.wahi9@gmail.com
